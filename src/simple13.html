Programs and puzzles

Here and on the next few pages, there are programs to try and puzzles to solve. They all involve BASIC commands covered in this book.

Words and spaces

Try this program. It makes the computer display words next to each other on the screen. 

10 CLS
20 PRINT "RED";"ROSE"
30 PRINT "BLACK";"COAL"
40 PRINT "WHITE" ;"SNOW"

See if you can change the PRINT lines to make spaces between the words, as shown in #2 below.

Can you change the PRINT lines again so the computer arranges the words in two columns like in #3?

Star lines

Run this program to make your computer display a line of stars at the side of the screen. 

10 CLS
20 FOR K=1 TO 10
30 PRINT TAB(3);"*"
40 NEXT K

By changing the number after TAB, can you put the stars in the middle of the screen?

Can you change the program again to make a line of stars across the screen, like this?
